Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our programs and learning experience

What prerequisites do I need for your courses?

Our beginner courses require no prior experience. For intermediate and advanced courses, we recommend basic familiarity with programming concepts or mathematics fundamentals. We offer free preparatory materials to help you get up to speed before starting any course.

How much time should I expect to spend on coursework each week?

Most students spend between 5-10 hours per week on coursework, depending on their pace and the specific course. Our flexible scheduling allows you to adjust your study time based on your availability.

Do you offer certificates upon completion?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all courses and programs. These certificates can be shared with potential employers or educational institutions.

Can I switch between plans after enrolling?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll gain immediate access to the additional features of the higher plan. If you downgrade, you'll retain access to completed coursework and certificates, but lose access to premium features of the higher plan.

What kind of support do you offer during the courses?

We offer support through WhatsApp / Telegram group for class support, weekly live classes with recordings, and progress reports to parents. Optional 1-on-1 mentorship will be available as a paid add-on in the future.
